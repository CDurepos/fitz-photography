"Hi, my name is Deklin and I am a Junior at the University of Southern Maine. Currently, I am a Media Studies Major with a Public Relations & Political Science minor.

I live with my cat, Cleo, in Southern Maine. When not in classes or at work; I can oftentimes be found with friends or behind my camera."

Deklin Fitzgerald is a professional photographer based in the breathtaking landscapes of Southern Maine. Hailing from the midcoast, his love for photography was born out of a desire to merge art with personal storytelling—creating images that serve as both a reflection and an exploration of the world through his unique lens.

Picking up his first camera in September 2022, photography has become more than just a passion; it’s his way of connecting deeply with people and places. Each click of the shutter is an opportunity to tell a story, to immortalize a moment in time that is rich with emotion and significance. Whether it’s the tender glance shared by newlyweds or the quiet confidence of a senior portrait, he strives to create images that resonate long after the moment has passed.

His expertise lies in individual and small group photography, with experience with headshots, senior photos, weddings, and event photography. Every session is an adventure, a collaboration where the aim is to capture not just faces, but the essence of those in front of his lens; in short he believes in creating memories. For Fitzgerald, the best photography comes from authentic interactions, where the emotions are genuine, and the experience is as memorable as the images themselves.
